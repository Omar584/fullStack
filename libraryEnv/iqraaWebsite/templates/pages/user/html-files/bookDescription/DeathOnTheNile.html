Death On The Nile - Agatha Christie

Book ID: 1014

Novel

Death on the Nile" is Agatha Christie's classic murder mystery set aboard a luxurious Nile steamer in Egypt. Detective Hercule Poirot investigates the murder of young heiress Linnet Ridgeway, unraveling a web of deceit, jealousy, and hidden motives among the passengers. With twists and turns, Poirot's keen observation and deduction lead to the revelation of the killer's identity against the backdrop of Egypt's stunning landscapes. Christie's masterful storytelling and intricate plotting make it a timeless classic in detective fiction.
