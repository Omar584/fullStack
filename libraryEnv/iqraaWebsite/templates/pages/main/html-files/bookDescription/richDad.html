Rich Dad Poor Dad - Robert T. Kiyosaki

Book ID: 1001

Personal-Finance

Rich Dad Poor Dad is a financial self-help book written by Robert Kiyosaki and Sharon Lechter. The book tells the story of a boy with two fathers, one rich and one poor, to help readers develop the mindset and financial knowledge needed to build a life of wealth and freedom. The titular "rich dad" is Kiyosaki's best friend's father who accumulated wealth due to entrepreneurship and savvy investing, while the "poor dad" is claimed to be Kiyosaki's own father who worked hard all his life but never obtained financial security
