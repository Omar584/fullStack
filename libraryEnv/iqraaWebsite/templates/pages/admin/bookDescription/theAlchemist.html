The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho

Book-ID : 1015

Novel
"The Alchemist" follows Santiago, a young shepherd who embarks on a journey to find a hidden treasure near the Egyptian pyramids after having a recurring dream. Along the way, he encounters various characters who impart wisdom about life and destiny. Through his journey, Santiago learns to follow his dreams and listen to the language of the world. Set against the backdrop of the desert, the novel explores themes of self-discovery, perseverance, and the pursuit of one's dreams.
