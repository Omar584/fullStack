Laws of Jartin

Amr Abdulhameed

Book-ID : 1016

Novel
"The Rules of the Neighbourhood" is a novel by the Egyptian author Amr Abdelhamid. The novel revolves around the lives of people in a popular neighborhood in Cairo, Egypt. It tackles various social, political, and economic issues individuals face in their daily lives, such as poverty, crime, corruption, and violence, and how people deal with these challenges and conflicts. Amr Abdelhamid's style is characterized by its realism and accurate portrayal of life in the popular neighborhoods, making this novel a powerful and impactful novel that sheds light on both the dark and bright aspects of urban life in Egypt.
