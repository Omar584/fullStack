Creativity

Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi

Book-ID : 1006

Philosophical study
The book examines the creative excitement experienced by artists, scientists, and exceptional individuals. Csikszentmihalyi interviewed over 90 people who have transformed their fields, exploring how creativity shapes their lives. He challenges the myth of the “tortured genius” and emphasizes the need to cultivate creativity not only in arts and sciences but also in business, government, and education. By studying the lives of these exceptional individuals, Csikszentmihalyi shows us how we can enhance our everyday lives through creativity
